My Hometown - Ranikhet

Ranikhet (queen's Meadow) is a hill station and cantonment town, in the Indian state of Uttarakhand. Ranikhet is one of the most popular attractions in Uttarakhand that is blessed with some of the best scenic vistas and natural beauty.

Places to visit in Ranikhet

  • Ranikhet Golf Course
  • KRC (Kumaon Regimental centre ) Museum
  • Haidakhan Babaji Temple
